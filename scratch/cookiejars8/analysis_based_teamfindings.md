##### IDEAL CAMPAIGN 
The campaign should run from September to April. Focus on existing customers, especially those who previously subscribed, as prior success is the strongest predictor of future conversion. Target customers who were contacted recently. Use 2–3 follow-ups; more than that reduces effectiveness. Calls should last 5+ minutes, as longer duration increases success. Contact method (cell vs telephone) does not significantly impact results.

##### IDEAL CLIENT
The ideal customer is financially stable, with an account balance above $1,428, no personal or housing loans, and no credit default. High-balance customers without debt have the highest conversion rates. Target individuals in blue-collar, management, technician, administrative, service roles, retirees, and students.
